Avacado Egg Toast Recipe

Delight in the flavors of a Chicken Pesto Wrap, featuring grilled chicken breast strips, vibrant pesto sauce, crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and gooey melted mozzarella cheese, all wrapped up in a soft tortilla for a satisfying lunch on the go or a quick and delicious dinner option.
