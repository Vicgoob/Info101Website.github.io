Welcome to The Cookbook!

We are Group 7, and for our project we decided to create a website which consolidates our favorite recipes for the four main meals of the day, Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and last but certainly not least Dessert! Through our navigation bar on the top of the screen you can find links to each page, one for each of the four meals chosen by the group member who created the page.
