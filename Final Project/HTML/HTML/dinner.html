Chicken Biryani Recipe

Biryani is one of the most renowned dishes from the Southeast Asian subcontinent. The basis of the meal revolves heavily around rice, spices, and some type of protein and vegetables. Marination, prep, and cooking can take a couple of hours, however, the meal is rich and fulfilling!

Potato Vegetable Curry Reciple

Another famous dish from this region is a Potato Vegetable curry that can be consumed on its own, or with something else on the side, such as bread. Unlike Biryani, Potato Vegetable curry can be made in about half an hour. All that is needed is a pressure cooker, the ingredients starting with Potatoes, followed by vegetables of choice such as garlic or pickles.
